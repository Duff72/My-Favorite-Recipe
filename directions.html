Directions

Step-by-Step Instructions to make Wings

Preparation

  1. Arrange wings in a single layer in a plastic container
  2. In a separate bowl, combine Salt, Garlic Powder, White Pepper, Paprika, optional Cayene, and Baking Powder
  3. Apply seasoning mix to wings, ensuring even coverage on each wing
  4. Cover and marinate in refrigerator at least 24 hours

Cooking

  1. Preheat oven to 400 FÂ°
  2. Arrange wings in a single layer on baking sheet and cook for 50 - 60 minutes, flipping halfway through
  3. While wings are baking, combine Hot Sauce and Melted Butter in a large bowl
  4. Remove wings from oven and toss in sauce, ensuring even coverage
  5. Serve alongside celery and Blue Cheese Dip

Step-by-Step Instructions to make Blue Cheese Dip

  1. Put 2 Tablespoons of Blue Cheese crumbles in a bowl
  2. Add the other ingredients to the bowl
  3. Whisk with a fork
  4. Gently fold in the remaining Blue Cheese crumbles
